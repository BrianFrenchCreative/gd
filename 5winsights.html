Implementing a new brand identity

A complete rollout across tradeshow, marketing, and promotional materials to ensure a unified brand experience.

  • The Challenge

    Following a company-wide brand refresh, a wide range of existing tradeshow and marketing materials were no longer aligned with the new visual identity and strategic messaging. This created a fragmented brand experience and diluted the impact of the new identity in the market.

  • My Role

    As the lead designer on this initiative, I was tasked with auditing all existing collateral and spearheading the effort to update every touchpoint to be consistent with the new brand standards.

  • The Process

    My process began with a comprehensive audit of all existing materials. I owned the responsibility of reviewing existing collateral, identifying necessary updates and polish to align with new brand standards and executing the changes.

    This involved:

    • Analysis: Carefully evaluating each piece, from a large-scale tradeshow backdrop to case studies, brochures and digital ads, to determine what could be updated versus what needed to be redesigned from the ground up.
    • Execution: I then moved into hands-on design, where I created original graphic assets and visual elements, integrating them into the robust and scalable design system I built to ensure brand cohesion. This ensured that every piece of collateral was not only visually updated but also built for future consistency and ease of use.
    • Collaboration: Throughout the project, I collaborated on internal branding and rebranding efforts with key marketing and leadership stakeholders to ensure the final output was perfectly aligned with the new strategic direction.

  • The Outcome

    The result was a cohesive suite of marketing and tradeshow materials that presented a strong, unified brand identity across all customer-facing touchpoints. The updated collateral successfully launched the new identity into the market and provided the team with scalable templates to maintain consistency moving forward.
